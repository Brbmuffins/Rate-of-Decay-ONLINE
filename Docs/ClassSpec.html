Rate of Decay Online

A post-collapse co-op experience set in the ruins of a technologically utopian world, shattered after a political movement to liberate its robots failed. Five playable classes. No leveling. Combat defines how the environment responds to you.

You think two fights ahead. Before the enemy arrives you've already placed the turret, mined the doorway, and charged the Arc Cannon. Your kit rewards preparation over reaction — the player who reads the map and sets the board wins before the first shot lands.

⚙
Passive ability
Overengineered
Your deployed objects accumulate output stacks every 4 seconds while you remain within 12 units. Each stack increases their damage or healing by 8%, up to 5 stacks. A turret at max stacks hits 40% harder. Staying near your work makes it stronger — and leaving lets it decay.
Abilities — equip any 4 of 7

Support · Circle · 6s cd
Deploy a salvaged Liberation-era combat drone. Auto-targets enemies on sight. Gains output stacks from your passive while you're nearby.
Arc Cannonexisting
Damage · Beam · 4s cd
Chargeable piercing beam. 15 to 50 damage depending on charge. Widens 1.8x at full — turns a surgical shot into a corridor of damage.
Shock Mineexisting
Damage · Trap · 5s cd
Invisible proximity charge. 40 burst damage on trigger. Stack them on chokepoints and doorways. The map becomes a weapon.
Overdriveexisting
Support · Aura · 12s cd
Instant self-cast aura — boosts all nearby ally damage output for 4 seconds. Call it before the team pushes, not during.
Drone Commandnew
Support · Target · 8s cd
Redirect your active Sentinel to focus a specific target for 6 seconds. The drone stops roaming. Use it on elites or priority targets your team is already focusing.
Field Repairnew
Heal · Targeted · 6s cd
Instantly restores a single ally's HP and removes one active debuff. Short range, no AoE. The Engineer's only direct heal — use it on your most critical teammate.
System Overloadultimate

All active deployables fire simultaneously at full output for 8 seconds. Instantly triggers max passive stacks on all of them. Best when you have 3+ objects on the field before casting.
T
Signature deployable
Engineer Turret
A reprogrammed Liberation-era combat drone. Targets enemies on sight, holds position until destroyed. Gains stacks from Overengineered passive. Engineer can have 3 active deployables simultaneously — all other classes are limited to 1.
How a round plays
1
Before combat — place Sentinel Drop at the chokepoint, Shock Mine on the secondary entrance. You've already won two decisions before the fight starts.
2
When enemies arrive — activate Overdrive for your team, then hold Arc Cannon charge while they push through your mines. High damage window with low personal risk.
3
If an elite emerges — Drone Command redirects your Sentinel onto it. Your turret and your beam are now focused on the same target. Priority kill without repositioning.
4
If the push succeeds — System Overload detonates everything simultaneously. Maximum passive stacks, maximum output. Clear the room and reset your board.
Best paired with
Guardian — Magnetize pulls enemies into your mine field
Phaser — Overdrive amplifies their burst window
Medic — Field Repair covers for Triage Loop gaps

You are the reason the run doesn't collapse. You're not there to deal damage — you're there to make the hardest fight survivable. Your decisions are about threat management: which enemy gets locked down first, when to absorb, when to call the push.

⬡
Passive ability
Threat Protocol
Taking damage generates Threat stacks. At 5 stacks, nearby enemies redirect aggro to you instead of allies for 6 seconds, and you gain 20% damage reduction for the duration. Dying clears all stacks. The Guardian who deliberately stands in fire keeps the run alive.
Abilities — equip any 4 of 7
Kinetic Reversalexisting
Counter · Cone · 10s cd
Absorb incoming hits for 3 seconds. Release stored kinetic energy as a 70° cone — up to 60 damage. The more you absorb during the window, the harder it hits.
Deflect Protocolexisting
Support · Instant · 8s cd
Hardlight barrier — absorbs 50 damage before HP is touched. In Guardian's hands this can be cast on an ally, not only self. Instant, no aim required.
Magnetizeexisting
Control · Anchor · 7s cd
Pulls all enemies in radius toward an anchor point. Zero damage. Pure setup — follow immediately with team AoE. Call it in voice before casting.
Breach Slamnew
Damage · Charge · 6s cd
Charge forward 6 units — 25 damage and staggers all enemies hit. Generates 3 Threat stacks instantly. Your gap closer and aggro pull combined.
Siege Modenew
Support · Stance · 14s cd
Go stationary for 6 seconds. Gain 40% damage reduction and 3x Threat generation rate. You cannot move or dodge. Maximum defense, maximum target priority.
Iron Tethernew
Control · Leash · 9s cd
Lock onto one enemy — they cannot move more than 8 units from you for 5 seconds. You absorb 15% of their attacks on allies during the tether. Counters flankers.
Last Bastionultimate
Support · Wall · 50s cd
Deploy a full-width hardlight barrier wall. Blocks all incoming projectiles. Your team shelters behind it. Holds for 10 seconds. The entire map changes when this goes up.
◈
Signature deployable
Bastion Node
A stationary hardlight emitter. Projects a dome that absorbs 30 damage per ally inside before they take HP damage. Lasts 20 seconds or until destroyed. Guardian can anchor a position around it.
How a round plays
1
Breach Slam into the largest group — instant aggro, stagger, 3 Threat stacks. You've pulled their attention before anyone else fires.
2
Activate Siege Mode in the middle of them — maximum Threat generation, damage reduction active. You absorb everything. Teammates attack freely.
3
Iron Tether on the flanker who breaks off toward the Medic. They're locked to you. The Medic lives.
4
If a boss ability fires — Last Bastion. Ten seconds of cover. The run doesn't end here.
Best paired with
Medic — Adaptive Shield on Guardian turns Siege Mode into almost unkillable
Engineer — Magnetize feeds Shock Mine detonations
Phaser — Magnetize clusters enemies for Event Horizon

You hit harder than anyone and you're never where they expect. Your passive demands that you cycle through your entire kit constantly to build Phase Charge — a Phaser who sits on one spell is leaving damage on the floor. The highest skill ceiling in the roster.

◎
Passive ability
Phase Charge
Every ability use charges a Phase meter (6 charges to fill). At full charge, your next offensive ability deals 40% bonus damage and the meter resets. Rewards rapid cycling — you cannot sit on one spell. Phase Shift casts do not consume meter, letting you reposition freely between charges.
Abilities — equip any 4 of 7
Dark Blastexisting
Damage · Cone · 3s cd
Chargeable 60° cone — 10 to 30 damage. Fastest cooldown in the kit. Good Phase Charge filler between heavy hitters.
Thermal Chargeexisting
Damage · Projectile · 4s cd
Plasma slug that detonates on impact. Chargeable AoE — 20 to 45 damage. Longest range AoE in the kit. Best cast after Phase Charge is full.
Neural Spikeexisting
Damage · Instant · 5s cd
35 flat damage, no charge needed. Best used at full Phase Charge for the 40% bonus — turns 35 into 49. Your primary finisher.
Phase Shiftnew
Mobility · Teleport · 4s cd
Teleport up to 10 units in the aimed direction instantly. Does not consume Phase Charge meter. Your escape, your reposition, your engage. Changes how you exist in space.
Singularitynew
Control · Pull · 9s cd
Deploy a micro black hole — pulls all enemies within 8 units toward center for 3 seconds, then collapses for 20 AoE damage. Sets up your own follow-up or a teammate's.
Arc Lancenew
Damage · Chain · 7s cd
Chain lightning that jumps between up to 4 enemies. First target takes 30 damage, each jump loses 5. At Phase Charge: 42 on first target. Best against grouped enemies.
Event Horizonultimate
Damage · Singularity · 50s cd
Massive singularity at target location. 12-unit pull radius, 3 second collapse, then 60 AoE burst. Survivors have exposed weakpoints for 8 seconds — all incoming damage increased by 25%.
◈
Signature deployable
Phase Relay
A phase-energy anchor that increases Phase Orb blast radius by 40% and extends Singularity pull duration by 2 seconds within a 10-unit area. Passive field — no activation needed. Enables larger AoE windows for the whole team while it's up.
Phase Charge rotation
1
Phase Shift into range — meter stays clean, you're in position. Charge 0 of 6.
2
Singularity down — enemies pulled together, meter charges. Dark Blast as they cluster. Arc Lance across the group. Charge 3 of 6.
3
Thermal Charge into the center as they collapse. Another Dark Blast fast. Charge 5 of 6. One more ability fires the bonus.
4
Neural Spike at full Phase Charge — 49 damage on the elite survivor. Phase Shift out before the counter-push. Full cycle, full reset.
Best paired with
Guardian — Magnetize clusters for Singularity, then Event Horizon finishes
Engineer — Overdrive active during Phase Charge burst doubles output
Medic — Phaser needs someone to keep them alive at low survivability

Without you the run ends. You're not a passive healer — you make active decisions about who lives and what you're willing to spend to keep them there. Transfer Protocol and Defibrillator are both high-stakes calls that define whether this team makes it out.

+
Passive ability
Triage Loop
Each ally you successfully heal generates a small self-repair on you — 8% of the heal amount feeds back into your own HP. A Medic who stays active stays healthy. One who waits for the right moment to heal will find themselves low with nothing left to give.
Abilities — equip any 4 of 7
Repair Waveexisting
Heal · AoE · 5s cd
Nanite cloud burst at target location. Heals all allies in a 3-unit radius. Aim it where the team is retreating to, not where they currently are.
Transfer Protocolexisting
Heal · Tether · 9s cd
Tether to an ally — their incoming damage routes to you for 5 seconds. They become untouchable. You take the hit. Use when an ally is too low to survive another strike.
Nanite Swarmexisting
Heal · Mobile · 7s cd
Mobile heal cloud that drifts toward target ally. Heals on arrival, chips enemies it passes through. Aim it through enemy lines for dual value.
Defibrillatorexisting
Heal · Context · 14s cd
Revives a downed ally at 30% HP — or deals 60 burst damage to robotic enemies. Same button. Reading the situation is the skill. Highest cooldown in the kit.
Adaptive Shieldnew
Support · Shield · 10s cd
Apply a shield to an ally that grows stronger as they take damage — scales from 20 to 80 absorb over 8 seconds of sustained fire. Best placed on the Guardian before they engage.
Purge Protocolnew
Utility · Cleanse · 7s cd
Remove all negative effects from an ally instantly. Clears slows, debuffs, damage-over-time effects. No damage, no heal — pure cleanse. The ability that makes some fights possible.
System Rollbackultimate
Heal · Rewind · 60s cd
Rewind the entire team 5 seconds. Restores HP and position, removes all debuffs. Effectively a team undo. The highest-cooldown ability in the game. When you use it, it should feel like a save.
+
Signature deployable
Restoration Beacon
A pulsing nanite emitter that passively heals all allies within 8 units every 3 seconds. Low output but persistent. Place it at the team's fallback point. Works with the Triage Loop passive — every pulse it triggers feeds back a small heal to the Medic.
How a round plays
1
Adaptive Shield on Guardian before they Breach Slam in. Their Siege Mode is now nearly unkillable.
2
Deploy Restoration Beacon at the team's cover position. Repair Wave the cluster when HP drops. Triage Loop keeps you topped off while you work.
3
Transfer Protocol on a low ally when they can't be healed in time. You take the hit. Purge Protocol on them immediately after if they're debuffed. Two-ability save sequence.
4
If the fight turns — System Rollback. 5 seconds back. Everyone has HP. Everyone is out of the fire. The run continues.
Best paired with
Guardian — Adaptive Shield on a Siege Mode Guardian is the strongest defensive combo
Phaser — keeps low-survivability burst damage alive
Wraith — Purge Protocol saves them from debuffs that break their stealth windows

You don't fight the same battle the rest of your team fights. You find the most dangerous enemy on the map and you make them stop existing. Then you disappear. You're not a tank, not a healer — you're the reason the hard fight suddenly got easier for everyone else.

✦
Passive ability
Bounty System
Each kill reduces all your ability cooldowns by 2 seconds. Landing the killing blow on an elite or named enemy reduces cooldowns by 5 seconds. You thrive in sustained combat — the more you kill, the faster you can kill again. Going down costs you everything you built.
Abilities — equip any 4 of 7
Neural Spikeexisting
Damage · Instant · 5s cd
35 instant damage. From Phase Cloak stealth, deals 50% bonus damage — 52 on opener. The standard assassin entry. Bounty System reset shortens the gap between engagements.
Stasis Waveexisting
Damage · AoE · 6s cd
Massive close-range cryo burst. 15 damage, huge radius. Your panic button when surrounded — or a finisher when cloaked and in close. High risk, high clear.
Shock Mineexisting
Damage · Trap · 5s cd
Proximity charge — 40 burst. Wraith can place them while Phase Cloaked. Set the trap, let the enemy walk into it, Bounty System fires on the kill.
Phase Cloaknew
Stealth · Utility · 10s cd
Go fully invisible for 4 seconds. Cannot attack while cloaked but can reposition, place mines, and fully reset enemy targeting. Breaking cloak with Neural Spike triggers the 50% bonus damage.
Null Fieldnew
Control · Suppress · 12s cd
Drop a field that suppresses all enemy abilities for 5 seconds. Enemies inside cannot cast. Charge enemies become dangerous the second this expires — plan your exit or your Collapse timing.
Arc Lancenew
Damage · Chain · 7s cd
Chain lightning — jumps between up to 4 enemies. 30 damage on first, -5 per jump. Good follow-up after Stasis Wave groups them or Null Field clusters a pack of suppressed enemies.
Collapseultimate
Damage · Detonate · 40s cd
Consume all active debuffs on every enemy in range — each debuff consumed deals 20 damage. After a full Null Field window, this can hit a single enemy for 100+ damage. The highest single-target burst in the game with setup.
◆
Signature deployable
Shadow Relay
A stealth anchor that extends Phase Cloak duration by 3 seconds while within 6 units, and reduces the Phase Cloak cooldown by 4 seconds per kill made while the relay is active. Turns the Wraith into a persistent stealth threat across an extended engagement.
The standard assassination combo
1
Phase Cloak — reposition behind the elite target. Place a Shock Mine between you and the group. You're invisible, they don't know what's coming.
2
Null Field on the group — all abilities suppressed for 5 seconds. They can't respond. The window is open.
3
Break cloak with Neural Spike — 52 damage on the elite (50% cloak bonus). Bounty System fires on the kill. Cooldowns drop. You can keep going.
4
Collapse while debuffs are still active. Arc Lance across survivors. Stasis Wave if they surround you. By the time they respond, the fight is already over.
Best paired with
Medic — Purge Protocol removes debuffs that break Phase Cloak windows
Guardian — Last Bastion covers the Wraith's retreat window
Engineer — Shock Mines placed by both classes stack for devastating chokepoints
